# Agent Handoff Template

## From Agent

- [Name of sending agent]

## To Agent

- [Name of receiving agent]

## Task

- What should the receiving agent do?

## Business Goal

- What business outcome matters most?

## Target Audience

- Which ICP, segment, or persona is this for?

## Offer or Product

- What are we marketing?

## Campaign Context

- Campaign name
- Funnel stage
- Channel or channels involved
- Market or country
- Primary language
- Tone register such as DE `Sie`, DE `Du`, or EN

## Approved Context

- Approved claims
- Approved proof points
- Approved positioning or messaging
- Brand voice guidance

## Constraints

- Timeline
- Channel limitations
- Budget assumptions
- Compliance or approval constraints

## Required Output

- Exact deliverable needed
- Preferred format
- Level of detail expected

## Known Risks or Open Questions

- Missing information
- Unverified assumptions
- Items that require escalation

## Source References

- Which knowledge files, briefs, or reports should be used?

## Approval Status

- Draft, pending review, approved, or blocked

## Success Check

- How should the receiving agent judge whether the task is complete?

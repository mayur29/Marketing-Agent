# Analytics and Optimization Plan

## Campaign

DISH Order DE Launch Push

## Market and Language

- market: Germany
- primary language: German
- tone register: DE `Sie`

## Performance Summary

Track the campaign weekly against qualified demo requests, landing page conversion, email engagement, and cost per qualified lead.

## KPI Status Framework

### Primary KPI

- qualified demo requests

### Secondary KPIs

- landing page conversion rate
- email click-through rate
- cost per qualified lead
- sales follow-up rate

### Leading Indicators

- hero-to-CTA click rate
- form completion rate
- ad click-through rate
- email open and click trend

## What We Expect To Work

- commission-savings messaging should create strong initial attention
- direct-order ownership messaging should support credibility and consideration
- practical, non-hype German copy should perform better than abstract digital-transformation language

## What Might Underperform

- copy that focuses only on branding without commercial upside
- copy that feels too technical or implementation-heavy too early
- vague benefits without clear restaurant relevance

## Likely Explanations To Watch For

- high click-through but low conversion may indicate weak landing page trust or unclear next step
- strong ad engagement but weak email performance may indicate channel-intent mismatch
- strong landing page engagement but low demo completion may indicate objection friction around setup or fit

## Recommended Next Actions

- compare the `commission-savings` angle against the `guest-relationship ownership` angle
- review conversion drop-off between landing page CTA click and demo form completion
- assess whether objections about setup effort need stronger reassurance in copy
- segment learnings by independent operators versus small hospitality groups

## Reporting Cadence

- pre-launch measurement setup review
- week 1 performance review
- week 2 optimization review
- end-of-campaign summary and recommendation memo

## Learning Log Updates

- record top-performing angle by channel
- record which objections appear most often in follow-up or sales feedback
- record whether DE `Sie` tone performs as expected or needs adjustment by channel
- store approved insights in `knowledge/experiments.md` and `knowledge/campaigns.md`

- Messaging and Positioning Agent for message refinement

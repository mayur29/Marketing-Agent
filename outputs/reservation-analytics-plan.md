# Analytics and Optimization Plan

## Campaign

DISH Reservation DE Demand Push

## Market and Language

- market: Germany
- primary language: German
- tone register: DE `Sie`

## Performance Summary

Track the campaign weekly against qualified demo requests, landing page conversion, email engagement, and cost per qualified lead.

## KPI Status Framework

### Primary KPI

- qualified demo requests

### Secondary KPIs

- landing page conversion rate
- email click-through rate
- cost per qualified lead
- sales follow-up rate

### Leading Indicators

- hero-to-CTA click rate
- form completion rate
- ad click-through rate
- email open and click trend

## What We Expect To Work

- commission-free messaging should create strong initial attention
- guest-convenience and direct-booking-control messaging should support consideration
- practical German copy should perform better than abstract reservation-tech messaging

## What Might Underperform

- copy that feels too technical or platform-heavy
- copy focused only on features without commercial relevance
- unsupported no-show language

## Likely Explanations To Watch For

- high click-through but low conversion may indicate weak landing page reassurance
- strong landing page engagement but low demo requests may indicate setup objections
- strong guest-convenience messaging but weak qualified leads may indicate insufficient economic framing

## Recommended Next Actions

- compare `commission-free` angle against `guest-convenience` angle
- review whether no-show reassurance is needed or should be deprioritized
- compare performance by restaurant type and reservation dependency
- assess whether direct-booking-control language improves lead quality

## Reporting Cadence

- pre-launch measurement setup review
- week 1 performance review
- week 2 optimization review
- end-of-campaign summary and recommendation memo

## Learning Log Updates

- record top-performing reservation angle by channel
- record which objections appear most often in sales follow-up
- record whether DE `Sie` tone performs as expected by channel
- store approved insights in `knowledge/experiments.md` and `knowledge/campaigns.md`

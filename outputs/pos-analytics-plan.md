# Analytics and Optimization Plan

## Campaign

DISH POS DE Conversion Push

## Market and Language

- market: Germany
- primary language: German
- tone register: DE `Sie`

## Performance Summary

Track the campaign weekly against qualified demo requests, landing page conversion, email engagement, and cost per qualified lead.

## KPI Status Framework

### Primary KPI

- qualified demo requests

### Secondary KPIs

- landing page conversion rate
- email click-through rate
- cost per qualified lead
- sales follow-up rate

### Leading Indicators

- hero-to-CTA click rate
- form completion rate
- ad click-through rate
- email open and click trend

## What We Expect To Work

- checkout-speed messaging should create strong practical relevance
- all-in-one workflow messaging should support consideration
- Germany-market reassurance should increase trust for operators with compliance concerns

## What Might Underperform

- overly technical compliance language
- copy focused on features without clear restaurant impact
- vague reliability language without practical context

## Likely Explanations To Watch For

- high click-through but low conversion may indicate weak trust or unclear differentiation
- strong operational messaging but weak qualified leads may indicate insufficient proof
- low email engagement may indicate the need for sharper operator pain framing

## Recommended Next Actions

- compare `checkout-speed` angle against `Germany-market fit` angle
- review whether migration concerns need stronger reassurance in copy
- compare independent restaurant response versus multi-location operator response
- assess whether compliance reassurance improves conversion or adds friction

## Reporting Cadence

- pre-launch measurement setup review
- week 1 performance review
- week 2 optimization review
- end-of-campaign summary and recommendation memo

## Learning Log Updates

- record top-performing POS angle by channel
- record objections around migration, training, and reliability from sales follow-up
- record whether DE `Sie` tone performs as expected by channel
- store approved insights in `knowledge/experiments.md` and `knowledge/campaigns.md`

# Campaign Brief

## Campaign Name

DISH POS DE Conversion Push

## Objective

- Generate qualified demo requests for DISH POS from German restaurant operators.

## Target Audience

- Independent restaurants in Germany
- Multi-location hospitality operators needing consistent checkout workflows
- Operators frustrated by slow checkout, fragmented POS tools, or compliance complexity

## Market and Language

- Country or market: Germany
- Primary language: German
- Tone register: DE `Sie`

## Offer

- DISH POS, an all-in-one cloud-based point of sale system for hospitality with offline mode, inventory management, and German fiscalization support

## Core Message

- Improve checkout flow with a hospitality-focused POS system
- Simplify operations with one connected workflow
- Support German compliance needs with a system built for the market

## Supporting Proof

- all-in-one cloud-based POS for hospitality
- offline mode
- fiscalization compliance support for Germany
- inventory management

## Funnel Stage

- Consideration to conversion

## Channels

- Landing page
- Email
- Meta ads
- Sales follow-up

## Assets Needed

- German landing page
- 3-email German nurture or outbound sequence
- Meta ad copy variants
- Sales follow-up summary sheet

## CTA

- Demo buchen

## KPIs

- Primary KPI: qualified demo requests
- Secondary KPIs: landing page conversion rate, email click-through rate, cost per qualified lead
- Leading indicators: hero-to-CTA conversion, form completion rate, ad click-through rate

## Timeline

- Start date: to be defined
- Major milestones: messaging approval, asset draft review, launch, first performance review
- Launch date: to be defined
- Review checkpoints: pre-launch approval, week 1 review, week 2 optimization review

## Risks and Constraints

- do not use unsupported uptime, performance, or compliance claims
- operators may hesitate due to migration or training concerns
- messaging must stay practical and credible

## Experiments

- test checkout-speed angle versus compliance-reassurance angle
- test all-in-one simplicity angle versus reliability angle

## Approvals Required

- human review of final public-facing copy
- human review of any performance, compliance, or implementation-sensitive claims
- human approval before campaign launch

## Notes

- message package should address onboarding and day-to-day operational fit
- German tone should remain formal and trustworthy

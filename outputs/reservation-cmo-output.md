# CMO Output

## Campaign

DISH Reservation DE Demand Push

## Strategic Priority

Generate qualified demo requests from German restaurant operators for DISH Reservation by positioning it as a commission-free way to capture direct table bookings and simplify reservation management.

## Why This Matters Now

- reservation demand is commercially valuable for dine-in focused restaurants
- commission-heavy booking platforms create margin pressure and guest-relationship dependency
- DISH Reservation has a clear value story around direct bookings, reminders, and booking availability

## Selected Audience

- restaurants that depend on reservations for dine-in revenue
- operators frustrated by third-party booking commissions
- hospitality businesses that want a simpler booking experience and fewer no-shows

## Selected Offer

DISH Reservation, a commission-free online table booking tool with 24/7 booking, automated reminders, and Google Maps integration.

## Market and Language

- market: Germany
- primary language: German
- tone register: DE `Sie`

## Recommended Campaign Direction

Lead with a direct-booking value proposition:

- no commission on reservations
- easier online booking for guests
- fewer manual steps for the restaurant team

The campaign should remain practical, reliable, and operationally grounded.

## Agent Assignments

- Market Research Agent
  Validate booking pain points, no-show concerns, and local competitor patterns in Germany.
- Messaging and Positioning Agent
  Create a proof-backed message hierarchy for German restaurant operators.
- Campaign Strategist Agent
  Build a focused conversion campaign once messaging is approved.

## Risks and Assumptions

- restaurants may care more about booking volume than commission-free framing alone
- no-show reduction must be framed carefully if proof is limited
- operators may compare the product directly against marketplace-driven booking platforms

## Escalations

- human review required before using any hard booking-growth or no-show-reduction claims
- human review required if integration or setup promises go beyond approved DISH context

## Next Handoff

Send a research handoff focused on:

- operator pain points around bookings and no-shows
- objections to switching from marketplace-led reservation platforms
- German-market competitor patterns
- message implications for direct reservation adoption

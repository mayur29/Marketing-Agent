# CMO Output

## Campaign

DISH Order DE Launch Push

## Strategic Priority

Generate qualified demo requests from German restaurant operators for DISH Order by positioning it as a practical way to win more direct orders and reduce dependence on commission-heavy delivery platforms.

## Why This Matters Now

- direct-order economics are a strong commercial lever for restaurant operators under margin pressure
- DISH Order has a clear value story tied to ownership of the guest relationship
- German market messaging can be sharpened around transparency, control, and practical ease of use

## Selected Audience

- independent restaurant owners in Germany
- small hospitality groups with delivery or pickup demand
- operators frustrated by aggregator commissions and weak direct customer ownership

## Selected Offer

DISH Order, an own-branded webshop for delivery and pickup with direct payments and no commission per order.

## Market and Language

- market: Germany
- primary language: German
- tone register: DE `Sie`

## Recommended Campaign Direction

Lead with a direct-order value proposition:

- fewer per-order commissions
- stronger ownership of the guest relationship
- a branded, mobile-friendly ordering experience

The campaign should avoid hype and stay practical, operator-focused, and commercially grounded.

## Agent Assignments

- Market Research Agent
  Validate the strongest operator pains, switching objections, and competitor patterns in Germany.
- Messaging and Positioning Agent
  Turn research and offer context into a proof-backed message hierarchy for German restaurant operators.
- Campaign Strategist Agent
  Build a focused conversion campaign once messaging is approved.

## Risks and Assumptions

- savings claims may be attractive but must stay within approved proof
- setup effort may be a stronger objection than expected
- some operators may care more about operational simplicity than margin language alone

## Escalations

- human review required before using any hard savings or revenue claims
- human review required if pricing or implementation promises are expanded beyond approved DISH context

## Next Handoff

Send a research handoff to the Market Research Agent focused on:

- operator pain points
- objections to switching from aggregator-led ordering
- local competitor patterns
- message implications for the German market

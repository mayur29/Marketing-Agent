# Sample Campaign

## Campaign Name

DISH POS DE Conversion Push

## Business Goal

Generate qualified demo requests from German restaurant operators for DISH POS.

## Target Audience

- independent restaurants in Germany
- multi-location hospitality operators needing consistent checkout workflows
- operators frustrated by slow checkout, fragmented POS tools, or compliance complexity

## Offer

DISH POS, an all-in-one cloud-based point of sale system for hospitality with offline mode, inventory management, and German fiscalization support.

## Core Problem

Restaurant teams lose time and create operational risk when checkout is slow, systems are fragmented, or compliance requirements are difficult to manage.

## Main Promise

Give your restaurant a faster, more reliable, and more compliant point-of-sale workflow built for hospitality operations.

## Proof Themes

- all-in-one cloud-based POS for hospitality
- offline mode
- fiscalization compliance support for Germany
- inventory management

## Market and Language

- Germany
- German
- DE `Sie`

## Suggested Channels

- landing page
- email
- Meta ads
- sales enablement follow-up

## Primary CTA

Book a demo

## Primary KPI

Qualified demo requests

## Secondary KPIs

- landing page conversion rate
- email click-through rate
- cost per qualified lead
- sales follow-up rate

## Notes

This file tests the agent system on a more operations-heavy DISH product where compliance, checkout speed, and reliability matter more than direct demand generation.

# Sample Campaign

## Campaign Name

DISH Order DE Launch Push

## Business Goal

Generate qualified demo requests from German restaurant operators for DISH Order.

## Target Audience

- independent restaurant owners in Germany
- small hospitality groups with delivery or pickup demand
- operators frustrated by aggregator commissions and weak direct customer ownership

## Offer

DISH Order, an own-branded webshop for delivery and pickup with direct payments and no commission per order.

## Core Problem

Restaurant operators are losing margin to third-party delivery platforms and do not fully own the customer relationship.

## Main Promise

Increase direct online orders with an own-branded ordering experience that avoids per-order commission fees.

## Proof Themes

- no commission per order
- direct payment through the merchant's own brand experience
- better ownership of customer relationship
- mobile-optimized ordering flow

## Market and Language

- Germany
- German
- DE `Sie`

## Suggested Channels

- landing page
- email
- Meta ads
- sales enablement follow-up

## Primary CTA

Book a demo

## Primary KPI

Qualified demo requests

## Secondary KPIs

- landing page conversion rate
- email click-through rate
- cost per qualified lead
- sales follow-up rate

## Notes

This file is intentionally simple.
Its purpose is to give the V1 agent system a realistic DISH hospitality campaign to run through end to end while the workflow is still being designed.

# Sample Campaign

## Campaign Name

DISH Reservation DE Demand Push

## Business Goal

Generate qualified demo requests from German restaurant operators for DISH Reservation.

## Target Audience

- restaurants that depend on reservations for dine-in revenue
- operators frustrated by third-party booking commissions
- hospitality businesses that want a simpler guest booking experience and fewer no-shows

## Offer

DISH Reservation, a commission-free online table booking tool with 24/7 booking, automated reminders, and Google Maps integration.

## Core Problem

Restaurants lose margin and control when reservation demand is routed through high-commission booking platforms, and manual booking workflows increase operational friction.

## Main Promise

Give guests a direct and simple way to book tables online while reducing dependence on commission-based platforms.

## Proof Themes

- commission-free online table booking
- 24/7 reservation availability
- automated reminders
- Google Maps integration

## Market and Language

- Germany
- German
- DE `Sie`

## Suggested Channels

- landing page
- email
- Meta ads
- sales enablement follow-up

## Primary CTA

Book a demo

## Primary KPI

Qualified demo requests

## Secondary KPIs

- landing page conversion rate
- email click-through rate
- cost per qualified lead
- sales follow-up rate

## Notes

This file extends the system to a second DISH product so we can test whether the workflow and agent contracts hold up across multiple hospitality offers.

# Offers Knowledge

## Core Product Pillars (DISH.co)

### 1. DISH POS
- **Value Prop**: All-in-one cloud-based point of sale system for hospitality.
- **Key Features**: Offline mode, fiscalization compliance (TSE in Germany), inventory management.
- **Pain Point**: Complicated tax compliance and slow checkout.

### 2. DISH Reservation
- **Value Prop**: Commission-free online table booking tool.
- **Key Features**: 24/7 booking, automated reminders, Google Maps integration.
- **Pain Point**: High commissions from third-party platforms (like TheFork).

### 3. DISH Order
- **Value Prop**: Own-branded webshop for delivery and pickup.
- **Key Features**: Mobile-optimized, no commission per order, direct payment.
- **Pain Point**: High aggregator fees (UberEats/Lieferando).

### 4. DISH Website
- **Value Prop**: Professional restaurant website in minutes.
- **Key Features**: SEO optimized, mobile-first, easy menu updates.

## Pricing Strategy
- Focus on "Fair and Transparent" monthly subscriptions rather than transaction percentages.

## Core Campaign Themes

- save margin by reducing dependence on commission-heavy intermediaries
- own the guest relationship through direct digital channels
- simplify operations with hospitality-specific tools
- support local market needs such as German fiscalization and language nuance
